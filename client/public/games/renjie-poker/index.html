Renjie Poker

GOAL: Create a higher 5-card poker hand than the dealer. Ties go to the dealer!


GAMEPLAY: Select a subset of the deck, then draw a random card. If the card belongs in your subset, it's added to your hand. Otherwise, it's added to the dealer's hand.


LOCK RULE: Once you select your subset, you cannot change it until you hit a card from that subset.


The game ends when you have 5 cards. The dealer then draws to 8 cards. Best 5-card hand wins!

Based on Poker Solitaire by paramt
